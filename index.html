📌 HOW TO USE

📏 Distance Mode:

  1. Click on the map to place the first point
  2. Click again to place the second point
  3. Distance will be calculated automatically

🎯 Mortar Mode:

  1. Toggle MORTAR MODE ON
  2. Click to place mortar position (🎯 icon)
  3. Click again to place target (💥 icon)
  4. Green circle = max range (800m)
  5. Red circle = min safe distance (100m)

✏️ Draw Mode:

  1. Click DRAW button
  2. Select tool (line, circle, text)
  3. Choose color
  4. Draw on the map
💡 Tip: Press R to reset, C to clear, H for help, M toggle mode, D draw panel
